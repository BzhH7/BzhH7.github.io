P5221 Product

这题好像可以用莫反做,但数论分块似乎会被卡

∏i=1n∏j=1nlcm(i,j)gcd(i,j)=∏i=1n∏j=1nijgcd(i,j)2=∏i=1n∏j=1nij×∏i=1n∏j=1ngcd(i,j)−2\prod_{i=1}^n\prod_{j=1}^n\frac{lcm(i,j)}{gcd(i,j)}=\prod_{i=1}^n\prod_{j=1}^n\frac{ij}{gcd(i,j)^2}\\=\prod_{i=1}^n\prod_{j=1}^nij\times\prod_{i=1}^n\prod_{j=1}^ngcd(i,j)^{-2} i=1∏n​j=1∏n​gcd(i,j)lcm(i,j)​=i=1∏n​j=1∏n​gcd(i,j)2ij​=i=1∏n​j=1∏n​ij×i=1∏n​j=1∏n​gcd(i,j)−2

首先看 ∏i=1n∏j=1nij\prod\limits_{i=1}^n\prod\limits_{j=1}^niji=1∏n​j=1∏n​ij

=∏i=1nin∏j=1nj=∏i=1ninn!=(n!)n×(n!)n=(n!)2n=\prod_{i=1}^ni^n\prod_{j=1}^nj=\prod_{i=1}^ni^nn!=(n!)^n\times(n!)^n=(n!)^{2n} =i=1∏n​inj=1∏n​j=i=1∏n​inn!=(n!)n×(n!)n=(n!)2n

接着尝试化简一下后面那一坨

∏i=1n∏j=1ngcd(i,j)=∏d=1nd∑i=1n∑j=1n[gcd(i,j)=d]=∏d=1nd∑i=1nd∑j=1nd[gcd(i,j)=1]\prod_{i=1}^n\prod_{j=1}^ngcd(i,j)=\prod_{d=1}^nd^{\sum\limits_{i=1}^n\sum\limits_{j=1}^n[gcd(i,j)=d]}\\=\prod_{d=1}^nd^{\sum\limits_{i=1}^{\frac{n}{d}}\sum\limits_{j=1}^{\frac{n}{d}}[gcd(i,j)=1]} i=1∏n​j=1∏n​gcd(i,j)=d=1∏n​di=1∑n​j=1∑n​[gcd(i,j)=d]=d=1∏n​di=1∑dn​​j=1∑dn​​[gcd(i,j)=1]

仔细观察一下指数,发现可以用莫反加数论分块,但时间复杂度就是 O(nn)O(n\sqrt{n})O(nn​), 有点勉强,所以可以考虑欧拉函数 ∑i=1n∑j=1i[gcd(i,j)=1]=∑i=1nϕ(i)\sum\limits_{i=1}^n\sum\limits_{j=1}^i[gcd(i,j)=1]=\sum\limits_{i=1}^n\phi(i)i=1∑n​j=1∑i​[gcd(i,j)=1]=i=1∑n​ϕ(i)

令 sum(n)=∑i=1nϕ(i)sum(n)=\sum\limits_{i=1}^n\phi(i)sum(n)=i=1∑n​ϕ(i) 那么上述式子的指数 =2×sum(⌊nd⌋)−1=2\times sum(\lfloor\frac{n}{d}\rfloor)-1=2×sum(⌊dn​⌋)−1

⇒ans=(n!)2n×∏d=1nd2×sum(⌊nd⌋)−1\Rightarrow ans=(n!)^{2n}\times\prod_{d=1}^nd^{2\times sum(\lfloor\frac{n}{d}\rfloor)-1} ⇒ans=(n!)2n×d=1∏n​d2×sum(⌊dn​⌋)−1

因为此题卡空间,数组不能开long long,所以需要用到欧拉定理 ac≡ac mod ϕ(m)(mod m)a^c\equiv a^{c~mod~\phi(m)}(mod~m)ac≡ac mod ϕ(m)(mod m), 这样就不需要开long long了
