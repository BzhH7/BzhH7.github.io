[国家集训队]Crash的数字表格 / JZPTAB

可以知道，题目中所求的是

∑i=1n∑j=1mlcm(i,j)=∑i=1n∑j=1mijgcd(i,j)\sum\limits_{i=1}^n\sum\limits_{j=1}^mlcm(i,j)=\sum\limits_{i=1}^n\sum\limits_{j=1}^m\frac{ij}{gcd(i,j)} i=1∑n​j=1∑m​lcm(i,j)=i=1∑n​j=1∑m​gcd(i,j)ij​

枚举所有约数

∑i=1n∑j=1mijgcd(i,j)=∑d=1min(n,m)∑i=1n∑j=1mijd[gcd(i,j)=d]=∑d=1min(n,m)d∑i=1⌊nd⌋∑j=1⌊md⌋ij[gcd(i,j)=1]\sum\limits_{i=1}^n\sum\limits_{j=1}^m\frac{ij}{gcd(i,j)}=\sum\limits_{d=1}^{min(n,m)}\sum\limits_{i=1}^n\sum\limits_{j=1}^m\frac{ij}{d}[gcd(i,j)=d]\\=\sum\limits_{d=1}^{min(n,m)}d\sum\limits_{i=1}^{\lfloor\frac{n}{d}\rfloor}\sum\limits_{j=1}^{\lfloor\frac{m}{d}\rfloor}ij[gcd(i,j)=1] i=1∑n​j=1∑m​gcd(i,j)ij​=d=1∑min(n,m)​i=1∑n​j=1∑m​dij​[gcd(i,j)=d]=d=1∑min(n,m)​di=1∑⌊dn​⌋​j=1∑⌊dm​⌋​ij[gcd(i,j)=1]

令 sum(n,m)=∑i=1n∑j=1mij[gcd(i,j)=1]f(k)=∑i=1n∑j=1mij[gcd(i,j)=k],F(k)=∑i=1n∑j=1mij[k∣gcd(i,j)]sum(n,m)=\sum\limits_{i=1}^n\sum\limits_{j=1}^mij[gcd(i,j)=1]\\f(k)=\sum\limits_{i=1}^n\sum\limits_{j=1}^mij[gcd(i,j)=k],F(k)=\sum\limits_{i=1}^n\sum\limits_{j=1}^mij[k|gcd(i,j)]sum(n,m)=i=1∑n​j=1∑m​ij[gcd(i,j)=1]f(k)=i=1∑n​j=1∑m​ij[gcd(i,j)=k],F(k)=i=1∑n​j=1∑m​ij[k∣gcd(i,j)]

⇒F(k)=∑k∣df(d)⇒f(k)=∑k∣dμ(⌊dk⌋)F(d)=∑k∣dμ(⌊dk⌋)∑i=1n∑j=1mij[d∣gcd(i,j)]=∑k∣dμ(⌊dk⌋)d2∑i=1⌊nd⌋∑j=1⌊md⌋ij\Rightarrow F(k)=\sum\limits_{k|d}f(d)\Rightarrow f(k)=\sum\limits_{k|d}\mu(\lfloor\frac{d}{k}\rfloor)F(d)\\=\sum\limits_{k|d}\mu(\lfloor\frac{d}{k}\rfloor)\sum\limits_{i=1}^n\sum\limits_{j=1}^mij[d|gcd(i,j)]=\sum\limits_{k|d}\mu(\lfloor\frac{d}{k}\rfloor)d^2\sum\limits_{i=1}^{\lfloor\frac{n}{d}\rfloor}\sum\limits_{j=1}^{\lfloor\frac{m}{d}\rfloor}ij ⇒F(k)=k∣d∑​f(d)⇒f(k)=k∣d∑​μ(⌊kd​⌋)F(d)=k∣d∑​μ(⌊kd​⌋)i=1∑n​j=1∑m​ij[d∣gcd(i,j)]=k∣d∑​μ(⌊kd​⌋)d2i=1∑⌊dn​⌋​j=1∑⌊dm​⌋​ij

令 g(i,j)=i×(i+1)2×j×(j+1)2g(i,j)=\frac{i\times(i+1)}{2}\times\frac{j\times(j+1)}{2}g(i,j)=2i×(i+1)​×2j×(j+1)​

⇒f(k)=∑k∣dμ(⌊dk⌋)d2g(⌊nd⌋,⌊md⌋)\Rightarrow f(k)=\sum\limits_{k|d}\mu(\lfloor\frac{d}{k}\rfloor)d^2g(\lfloor\frac{n}{d}\rfloor,\lfloor\frac{m}{d}\rfloor) ⇒f(k)=k∣d∑​μ(⌊kd​⌋)d2g(⌊dn​⌋,⌊dm​⌋)

⇒sum(n,m)=f(1)=∑d=1min(n,m)μ(d)d2g(⌊nd⌋,⌊md⌋)\Rightarrow sum(n,m)=f(1)=\sum\limits_{d=1}^{min(n,m)}\mu(d)d^2g(\lfloor\frac{n}{d}\rfloor,\lfloor\frac{m}{d}\rfloor) ⇒sum(n,m)=f(1)=d=1∑min(n,m)​μ(d)d2g(⌊dn​⌋,⌊dm​⌋)

ans=∑d=1min(n,m)dsum(⌊nd⌋,⌊md⌋)ans=\sum\limits_{d=1}^{min(n,m)}dsum(\lfloor\frac{n}{d}\rfloor,\lfloor\frac{m}{d}\rfloor) ans=d=1∑min(n,m)​dsum(⌊dn​⌋,⌊dm​⌋)
